Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

IP Scanner Advanced does not collect or store any personal data. The app operates locally on your device to scan network connections.

2. Data Usage

The app only scans devices on your local network to display their IP addresses, MAC addresses, and vendor information. No data is transmitted to external servers.

3. Permissions

To function correctly, the app may request network-related permissions. These are solely used for scanning and analyzing network connections.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with third-party services for data collection. However, vendor identification may rely on publicly available MAC address databases.

5. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Please check periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at nrahmatd@gmail.com.
