Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

IP Scanner Advanced collects certain information to improve user experience and app functionality:

  • Advertising ID: We collect your device's Advertising Identifier (AAID/IDFA) for analytics purposes to understand app usage patterns and improve our services.
  • Network Information: The app scans devices on your local network to display their IP addresses, MAC addresses, and vendor information.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with the app to improve performance and user experience.

2. Data Usage

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To analyze app usage and improve our services
  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To develop new features and enhancements
  • To understand user preferences and optimize content delivery

The Advertising ID is used solely for analytics and is not shared with third-party advertisers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share aggregated, anonymized analytics data with:

  • Analytics providers to help us understand app usage patterns
  • Service providers who assist in app maintenance and improvement

Vendor identification may rely on publicly available MAC address databases.

4. Permissions

To function correctly, the app may request:

  • Network-related permissions for scanning and analyzing network connections
  • Access to your device's Advertising ID for analytics purposes

5. Data Retention

We retain analytics data for up to 12 months, after which it is either anonymized or deleted.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Please check periodically for any changes.
